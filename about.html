About Me

The Gist

Hi, I am Pramet Jaiin, a User Experience design student at MIT WPU. I'm also a coder, writer, and side project creator who enjoys diving into the world of design. In addition to my studies, I do web development and design. I am fluent in major design software and enjoy creating impactful user experiences. I believe in taking things one step at a time and am always eager to learn more.

Student Life

Right now, I’m working on Runtales, an audio-tainment fitness app that uses storytelling and gamification to make running more entertaining. It’s all about keeping runners consistent and motivated. This project is a chance for me to showcase my vision of making fitness fun and interactive.

I’m also doing freelance work with "I Can Design Studio" in Pune, helping brands with web development and design. I write articles on Medium about my experiences as a student and freelance designer, which has been a great way to share my journey and insights.

Personal Life

Outside of work, I’m a big reader and tech enthusiast. I love photography and videography, capturing everyday moments in unique ways. I’m a plant lover and an experimental cook—I enjoy trying new recipes and surprising friends with my cooking. When I’m in the mood, I like to write poems too.

I’m always on the lookout for new ideas, especially in fields like automobiles, motorbikes, luxury fashion, and tech. I’m eager to learn and ready for new challenges. If you’re looking for someone who’s creative, curious, and dedicated, I’d love to be a part of your team!
